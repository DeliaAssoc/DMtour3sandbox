Patients receive an email when care coordinators share health education materials with them. Let’s use Carol as an example. From the email, Carol is prompted to log into her Salesforce Community to access the materials and interact with her care team.
After logging in, Carol can access patient education resources from the Healthwise Inbox.
Carol can see the notes sent to her by her care team and access the education.
Carol can read important information about her health.
She can also watch short, engaging videos.
From the Healthwise Inbox, Carol can respond back to her care team.
Back in Salesforce Health Cloud, Carol’s comments and questions are captured in the Collaboration tab within Health Cloud. Everyone on the care team can see what resources were shared along with her feedback.
